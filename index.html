About Us

Yo bites cafe is more than just a café – it's a gathering place in the heart of Mekelle. We pride ourselves on serving authentic Ethiopian coffee alongside our signature premium gelato, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all our guests.

Whether you're looking for a quiet place to work, meet friends, or simply enjoy a sweet treat and exceptional coffee, Yo bites cafe is your home away from home.
